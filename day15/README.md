The program takes marks of up to 10 students and stores them in an array.
It calculates the total,average,highest,and lowest marks.
It counts how many students passed (marks>=50) and failed.
It classifies students into grades A+,A,B,C,D,or Fail based on their marks.
Finally,it displays a summary showing average,top/bottom marks,pass/fail count,and grade distribution.
